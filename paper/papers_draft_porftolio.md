## Cross-Paper Rules

- Keep the book as motivation, not as missing context.
- Do not let any paper say `we find` unless the result is actually present in the repo and summarized in the file.
- Remove all portfolio-era scaffolding from the manuscript body before submission.
- Keep the target construct narrow: benchmark, access, reanalysis, feasibility, or conceptual commitment.
- Treat `05_discriminative_program.md` and `07_qualia_back_action.md` as companion papers.
